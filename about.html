About Me

I was a civil engineer. Engineering projects start with detailed planning. Design proceeds with frequent reviews. Problems are overcome using logic and creative thinking. The result is a set of detailed documents describing how to reach a desired goal.

The creation of web apps is much like engineering design. An efficient, logical approach can be used in coding and developing both the front and back end technologies. Implementing a well-prepared plan results in a successful product.

As a web developer, I bring a wealth of experience in bringing projects to life. Teamwork is critical. My tools are different, and there are always new things to learn. But the goal is the same. A well-planned, well-executed process, with innovative thinking applied, brings new ideas to life.
