About Me

I was a civil engineer. Engineering projects start with planning. Design proceeds with frequent reviews. Problems are overcome by teams. The process of web development is very similar. Taking projects from planning to construction was my primary role.

The creation of web apps is like engineering design. A well thought out plan is applied when developing both the front and back end. Implementing a well-prepared plan results in a successful product.

As a web developer, the tools I use are different. I enjoy learning new skills in whatever field I work in. And, as a hobby photographer, now I can use my own images in my projects!
